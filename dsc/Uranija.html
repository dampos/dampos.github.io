Uranija

"URANIJA" is a renowned restaurant located in the heart of the city park, surrounded with beautiful vegetation. Visiting this restaurant means experiencing moments of very pleasant and relaxed ambiance, enriched with the sounds of international music. Their restaurant is recognized as one of the best restaurants in Macedonia for more than one decade. One visit to this restaurant is enough to realize that it is classified among the top of the most refinement restaurants, not only in Skopje, but also on the Balkans. This restaurant is a place visited by many world famous politicians and showbiz members, when they stayed in Skopje.
